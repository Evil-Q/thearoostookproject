Hi! I'm Evie. I'm a crazy christian cartoonist! I created the Aroostook Project and this website. I think one day I'll make a website for all my other stuff.

I have a comic in the school newspaper called Adventures in Guillemot. I like birds/birding, the Hoosiers, the Ink Spots, the Nevermoor series, and Ace Attorney (my current favorite thing). I also enjoy reading, sewing, coding, rock climbing, swimming (go Girls Swim and Dive 2025!), science, and math.

I use paper, pencil, gel pen, colored pencils, and a paint marker to make traditional art, including most Aroostooks and Adventures in Guillemot. I edit traditional art, add backgrounds to it, and occasionally make digital art in Procreate. I make 3D models in Blender and code in Visual Studio Code.
